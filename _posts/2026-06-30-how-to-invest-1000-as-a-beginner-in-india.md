Starting your investment journey can feel overwhelming, especially when you think you need a lot of money to begin. The truth is, you can start investing with as little as ₹1000 in India.

## Why Starting Small is Better Than Not Starting at All

Most people wait until they have enough money to invest. But this thinking costs you years of compound growth.

Consider this simple example:
- ₹1000 invested every month at 12% annual return becomes ₹35 lakhs in 20 years
- The secret is not the amount, it is starting early and staying consistent

## How to Start a SIP with ₹1000

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is the easiest way for Indians to start investing with small amounts.

1. Download Groww or Zerodha Coin app
2. Complete your KYC using Aadhaar and PAN card
3. Search for Nifty 50 Index Fund
4. Set up a monthly SIP of ₹1000
5. Link your bank account and activate

That is it. Your money will automatically invest every month.

You do not need a lot of money to start investing. ₹1000 is all it takes. Start today and your future self will thank you.
